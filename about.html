What is the Weekly Filthy Casual Report?

Weekly Filthy Casual Report was started to assist with not only casual players, but the everyday players with the idea of where they can quickly obtain information needed to play Destiny the Game. Whether that information is how to solo this week’s Nightfall or a guide on How-to Solo Crota’s End we want to have that information in one place so you can spend less time searching for information and more time playing.


Do you have an App?

Sort of, but not in the traditional sense you maybe thinking. If you add www.destinyfcr.com to the homescreen of your smart phone you'll have the app version. (How?)
