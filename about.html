About Weekly Filthy Casual Report

Weekly Filthy Casual Report was started to assist with not only casual players, but the everyday players with the idea of where a player can obtain quick and easy information. Whether that information is how to solo this week’s Nightfall or a guide on How-to Solo Crota’s End we want to have that information in one place so you can spend less time searching for information and more time playing.
