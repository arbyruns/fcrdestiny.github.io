What are Exotics

Exotics are an item of rarity. A player can only equip one weapon and one armour exotic at a time. Exotic items usually have some effects that are unique to certain subclass of characters. Exotics can be obtained through a various ways:

  • Purchase Exotics weapons and armour through Xur using strange coins.
  • Players can obtain Exotic items from enemy drops, high-level mission rewards, The Crucible, and decoding of engrams.
  • Players can also obtain Exotic weapons from the Exotic Bounties.
