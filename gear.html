Engrams

Engrams are loot that the Cryptarch can for you at the Tower. The Cryptarch will decrypt the engram free of cost and derpyted can yeild a one rarity above, below or of the same color of the engram

  • Green - Decoded in the field. Uncommon or rare.
  • Blue - Rare item
  • Purple - Available from Postmaster when a player reaches a new reputation level or found within the world. These can produce a legendary or exotic item
  • Gold - Exotic Engram Bought from Xur for 23 Motes of Light.

Exotics

Exotics are an item of rarity. A player can only equip one weapon and one armour exotic at a time. Exotic items usually have some effects that are unique to certain subclass of characters. Exotics can be obtained through a various ways:

  • Purchase Exotics weapons and armour through Xur using strange coins.
  • Players can obtain Exotic items from enemy drops, high-level mission rewards, The Crucible, and decoding of engrams.
  • Players can also obtain Exotic weapons from the Exotic Bounties.

Xur

Who is Xûr?

Xûr, Agent of the Nine is a merchant within the Tower. Xûr sells exotic weapons, armor, and consumables in exchange for Strange Coins and Motes of Light. He can be found around the Tower in different locations every weekend.

Xûr is also important to several Exotic Weapon Bounties, requiring Guardians to speak with him or purchase some of his wares to continue to the next stage of the bounties.

What's the best way to earn Strange Coins?

The best way to earn Strange Coins is by completing the Weekly Heroic Strike on all 3 difficulties. Each difficulty awards you (3) Strange Coins upon completion. Completing the Weekly Heroic Strike on the hardest difficulty earns you all (9) Strange Coins in one go. You can only earn (9) Strange Coins per Character per week from the Weekly Heroic. Assuming you have 3 Characters, you can earn a total of (27) Strange Coins by doing what's mentioned above on each Character.

Exotic Upgrades?

Xûr now sells exotic upgrades for Armor and Weapons. Each week he'll have a total of twelve upgrades. In order to obtain an upgrade, a Guardian must trade in the original Exotic piece, an Exotic Shard, and 7,000 Glimmer.

When is he at the tower?

You can check if Xur is at the tower by going to Destiny Public Event or Is Xur At the Tower.
